    <h1>Artificial Intelligence (AI)</h1>

    <p>
        Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to think, learn, and make decisions like humans.
        It is one of the most important technologies in today's world.
    </p>

    <h2>Uses of AI</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Robots and automation</li>
        <li>Self-driving cars</li>
        <li>Smart assistants like Siri and Alexa</li>
        <li>Medical diagnosis</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Advantages of AI</h2>
    <p>
        AI helps to reduce human effort, increase accuracy, and save time. It can work 24/7 without getting tired.
    </p>

    <h2>Disadvantages of AI</h2>
    <p>
        AI may replace human jobs and can be expensive to develop. It also depends on data and programming.
    </p>

    <h2>Conclusion</h2>
    <p>
        AI is a powerful technology that is changing the world. It should be used wisely for the benefit of society.
    </p>
